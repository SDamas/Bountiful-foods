We're proud of our history

Bountiful Foods was founded in the mid-1900s by a group of farmers who were committed to using sustainable, eco-friendly practices in their operations. The company began as a small cooperative, selling locally grown fresh fruits and vegetables to the residents of the California coast.

As demand for Bountiful Foods' products grew, the company expanded its operations to include a processing facility where it could produce fruit drink mixes and other value-added products. The company's dedication to using only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients quickly made it a favorite among consumers.

In the decades that followed, Bountiful Foods continued to innovate and diversify its product offerings, introducing a range of fruit-based snacks and other healthy food options. The company's commitment to sustainability and the use of local ingredients remained at the forefront of its business model.

Today, Bountiful Foods is a leading player in the California food industry, with a reputation for producing high-quality, delicious products that are good for both people and the planet. The company operates a network of farms, processing facilities, and distribution centers, and is dedicated to supporting the local economy and community.
