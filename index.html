Carlsbad. Our city.

Carlsbad is a city located in the northern part of San Diego County, California. It is situated along the Pacific Ocean and has a population of around 110,000 people.

The city is known for its beautiful beaches and resorts, as well as its proximity to other popular tourist destinations such as Legoland California and the Flower Fields at Carlsbad Ranch.

The economy of Carlsbad is driven by tourism, as well as by the presence of several major technology and biotechnology companies in the area. The city has a Mediterranean climate, with warm, dry summers and mild, wet winters.
